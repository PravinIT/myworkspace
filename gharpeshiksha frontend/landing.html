Frequently Asked Questions



Is Trial Class Free or Paid ?

Trial Class can be a free or paid class depending on tutor. In most cases, Trial Class is free of cost However in some cases experienced tutor might demand paid Trail Class.

To Whom I need to Pay ?

You can pay directly to tutor or You can also pay on our website. We will settle the amount with teacher only after asking you.

Will I have to pay in advance or Can I pay Session Wise ?

In monthly charge, payment is usually in done in advance(Recommends payment on our website) and in session wise class payment is usually done session wise.

Will I get the teacher's documents ?

Yes, We can show teacher's relevant documents on demand.

Will my fees amount be refunded if I do not wish to continue ?

If payment is made through us, Yes we will refund your amount on pro-rata basis within 2 working days.

What happen if teacher discontinue class in between ?

If payment is made through us, Your amount will be refunded on pro-rata basis or other tutor will be arranged.

Will I get fees payment receipt ?

If payment is made through us, Yes we provide payment receipt on each and every fees payment.
